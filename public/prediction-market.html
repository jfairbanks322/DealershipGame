Forecast Desk Brief

Why this matters

Prediction markets can be useful because they compress a lot of information into one live number. They can also become dangerous when people stop asking “What is true?” and start chasing adrenaline, identity, or a quick comeback after a loss.

  • Start with a price, but interrogate the reason behind it.
  • Notice when a market is reacting to evidence versus pure crowd energy.
  • Watch your sizing. Overconfidence can turn a forecast into a gamble.
Classroom policy

To help educate on the hazards of gambling, your grade for this activity will reflect how responsibly and effectively you manage the live class market.
