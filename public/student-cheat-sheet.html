Winning the current event is not enough. Your choices also shape the next event through morale, trust, and restaurant state.

1. Get In

Login and Setup

  1. Create your account with your display name, username, password, and manager portrait the first time you play.
  2. Use the same login each class so your restaurant progress stays saved.
  3. Do not share your password unless your teacher resets it for you.
If you cannot get in: ask your teacher to reset your password. Your restaurant data can still stay intact.

2. Read The Screen

What You Are Looking At

Restaurant State = long-term health Case Chain = current event Staff Cards = morale + trust Leaderboard = class ranking Decision Log = what you already did
  • Restaurant State shows the long-run condition of your business.
  • Lingering Effects show what is still following you from earlier events.
  • Staff trust and morale matter because broken relationships can eventually end your game.

3. Work The Case

How Each Event Works

  1. Read the event headline and context before clicking anything.
  2. Choose a staff member to consult based on who knows that problem best.
  3. Pick a response option that fits both the current crisis and the long-term health of the restaurant.
  4. Repeat through the full 6-step chain until the event resolves.
Important: a choice that looks good for revenue right now can still damage trust, morale, or your next event.

4. Score Smart

What Actually Matters

  • Revenue matters most in your aggregate score.
  • Morale matters next because a burned-out team gets harder to manage.
  • Trust still matters because low-trust employees weaken future decisions.
  • Restaurant State matters because it changes how later events behave.

5. Avoid Losing

How Students Fail The Game

  • If an employee crashes to zero morale or trust, they can quit.
  • If someone quits, your restaurant run is over until the teacher resets standings.
  • Ignoring the same weak area repeatedly can make future events much harder.

6. Simple Routine

Best Way To Play In Class

  1. Check your current restaurant state first.
  2. Read the full event carefully.
  3. Choose staff intentionally, not randomly.
  4. Watch for repeated damage to the same employee or weak system.
  5. Finish the full chain before class moves on.
